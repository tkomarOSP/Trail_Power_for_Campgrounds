## Summary

A formal Request for Proposal was generated for bulk procurement of Panasonic NCR18650B lithium-ion cells as specified in the Trail Power for Campgrounds Physical Architecture model. The cells are allocated to the `Store Energy` function within the `Battery Holder` component (child of `Charge Box`).

## Scope

- **Part:** Panasonic NCR18650B, 3400 mAh, flat top, unprotected
- **Quantity Tiers:** 100 / 500 / 1,000 / 5,000 cells
- **Key Requirements:** Authenticity certification (CoC), UN38.3 transport compliance, SDS, authorized distributor or traceable supply chain
